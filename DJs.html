DJs & HOSTING


DLE represents a number of first class DJs, all of whom are experienced, resident comperes and hosts at corporate hotels in the UK or venues across Europe. All come with first class sound and lighting equipment, are always immaculately presented for the occasion, and are consummately professional in their preparations beforehand for any requirements, running orders or special requests your party may have.



Dave Wilde

Dave has a fantastic musical knowledge and an insatiable appetite for fun. Brilliant for any event or themed night, Dave has been a resident hotel and corporate DJ for the past decade.


Pete Webster

A young, fresh, party DJ suitable for any occasion. Pete has performed as a resident DJ for many corporate hotels in the NW and the Midlands.




Colin Parry

A true master of ceremonies, Colin is a first class DJ and compere in addition to being an excellent singer, should you require something special for your evening's entertainment. A star of stage and screen, Colin is a successful actor, having appeared in Coronation Street, EastEnders and Hollyoaks.



Damien Davies

Young, full of fun, and with a great personality, DJ Damo is a perfect party DJ who specialises in 18th and 21st birthday parties, dance and R&B nights.



Ben Turley

Ben is an exceptionally talented and experienced DJ, host and compere, bringing fun and interaction to every single night. During the summer months, Ben takes up residence as the manager and host of Ibiza's biggest cabaret venue, Charlie's Bar, but is available for bookings between October and May in the UK.
