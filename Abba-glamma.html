    Abba Glamma - The UK's No.1 Tribute to Abba
    Available as a mixed four-piece or female duo tribute show

    Abba Glamma has been entertaining audiences across the UK and overseas since 2003 with its highly energetic and authentic tribute to the Swedish pop sensations. The two highly experienced vocalists, Louise Jaymes and Kelly Allen, capture the true essence of Abba and 1970s glamour with their amazing dance routines, exceptional vocal talents, stunning costumes and authentic performance. Quite simply not to be missed.

    Abba Glamma can also be booked as a four-piece band for the full, theatrical experience.
